Plan your path to Financial Independence.

FIRE calculator, Monte Carlo simulations, asset allocation, expense and net-worth tracking — all in one open-source toolbox. Runs in your browser, on your desktop, or self-hosted. Your data never leaves your device unless you decide otherwise.

Open the web app Download desktop View on GitHub

What you get

FIRE calculator

Project years-to-FIRE from savings rate, expenses, returns and inflation. Share scenarios via URL.

Monte Carlo

Probabilistic projections with configurable volatility and black-swan events.

Asset allocation

Track portfolio breakdown and get buy / sell / hold rebalancing hints.

Expense & net-worth tracking

Lightweight ledgers with CSV import/export — your bank, your rules.

Privacy-first

Client-side only by default. Sensitive data lives in AES-256-encrypted cookies on your device.

Self-hostable

A local backend (SQLite-first, Postgres-ready) ships via Docker Compose for power users.
